The Band Story



Long, long ago in a far, away galaxy !




Well yeah, it seems it. Relentless touring and gigging, the longest ‘apprenticeship’ ever, years of other ‘project’ bands, albums, families and then “THE VOID!!!” so why not, yeah. We is “OLD” as the kids say but if you’ve still got “THE PASSION, THE LOVE and the AMBITION” then why not!!!


After a chance meeting Ron and Al talked (could we, maybe?) and then did re-connect musically. Same old tastes but the essence as we all know is the entertainment factor.


So how do you ‘re-jig’ for a current music scene? Well, as once said by someone a lot smarter than us “the art is in the craft”. So maybe use what you know to the best of your abilities, play tunes that folk know, have fun and don’t confuse the issue. Get off your ‘High Horse’ and entertain, laugh, be friendly and “SMILE!”


HRB have tunes old and new and also venture into writing and performing “ORIGINAL MUSIC” but the mainstay for here and now is giving the audience something a bit different to ‘the old menu’ and putting it all together into a show but most of all...


“ENTERTAINING THE CROWD”



Meet the band

Say hello to ‘Ronnie R’. Ron first started playing live with Jack in the early 70’s, gigging and performing sometimes up to 5 nights a week. All changed when the band embarked on the MECCA touring circuit with the band playing month after month, thirty nights straight (and 1 night to travel!) This experience led Ronnie to be a self-confessed “gig-a-holic!”

After years on the commercial touring circuit Ron signed, recorded and toured Europe with UK bands ‘Stormwatch’ and ‘Wytchfynde’ playing Rock music to excited fans! However in HRB Ron takes shared lead vocals and harmonies in a more subdued role. But as a well-known ‘guitar slinger’ is no slouch to providing excitement and entertainment learned from years of playing all over the northern hemisphere!

Here’s Alan (Jack) Bunker, one of the original members of ‘THE HARVEY RUFF BAND’. Starting off like so many Sheffield Musicians playing and gigging (along with Ron) in the mid 70’s pub and club scene. Later going on to tour Scandinavia on the once vibrant MECCA circuit.

Later, after parting ways with cover bands Jack found his ’15 minutes of fame’ with the Sheffield based band “Phoenix Rising” and toured extensively supporting 1st division bands the like of “Marrillion” and “Wishbone Ash” and has performed to countless thousands during his long and varied showbiz career.

With a voice likened to Paul Young, Rod Stewart and Paul Rodgers and as a Drummer vocalist Jack brings along something a little unique and entertaining. ‘Always the joker’ Jack brings a friendly, relaxing vibe to every venue.

Introducing Dan Oakley, sound and recording engineer, technical adviser and HRB’s Bass Guitarist who also ‘features’ on backing vocals.

Dan brings his experience to the table arranging and developing different ways to bring ‘old light through new windows’. Making older covers seem refreshed and maybe more fun than when they were originally chart toppers!

Forming a formidable engine along with Jack, Dan provides the invaluable ‘backbone’ needed in a totally live Three piece band having toured extensively around the UK and played alongside some of the greats of our time. Dan also brings a warm, friendly, polite attitude to your venue.
